Welcome Freshers

College is a place which is confronted with rich diversity of different people, new ideas and new opportunities for personal growth. It is a time when one begins to ask themselves several basic questions: who am I, what’s important to me, what do I want from life, how do I achieve it? These are some of the questions which all of us come across at some point during our college years.
College is a time of change and adjustment. Many of you must be leaving your families and friends behind to seek out new relationships and create an identity for yourself on campus. There will be increased freedoms but with that will come more responsibility. As you move forward towards your life goals, you will begin to separate from family and learn to stand on your own.
College will bring increased academic demands and expectations .In order to be successful you need to refine your study habits, learn to prioritize your task, manage your time effectively and cope up with the stress.
The next four years will be the most cherished years of your life and make sure that you make the best use of years to come. Welcome to UPES, your home for the next four years. So try to get best out of these years and have a very happy college life ahead.

About Us

UPES CSI Student Chapter is one of the most active chapters of University of Petroleum and Energy Studies and aims to live by its motto CREATE SHARE INNOVATE. The Student chapter was established in the year 2010 with a handful of member .With the passage of time the membership has risen up to 650 plus students. The chapter organizes many technical and fun events, seminars, conferences all of which have a healthy participation from the students. CSI Student Chapter has successfully organised a State Convention which had over 400 participants, Regional Convention having more than 600 participants. This year UPES CSI organized a National Student Convention which had a footfall of 750 plus students and professionals from the industry. CSI Student chapter hosted Technical Premier League which encompassed a reasonably wide variety of participants as well as organizers. It comprised of a fairly comprehensive group of events that encouraged participants across many verticals enhancing the debating, photography, coding and designing skills which are the essence of any computer science students. The chapter also lays emphasis on social work and issues and has tried to spread the awareness .The chapter involves in CSR activities and works towards making Dehradun “Computer Literate” by organizing study camps for under privileged students. UPES CSI organised an event “Human The Virtue” which provided a platform to the student of Clodine International School to showcase their skills. UPES CSI has evolved way past it’s years and now, is ardently recognized as one of the best student chapter for the second time in a row. It has even been awarded the highest committed student activist award. The chapter aims to attain greater heights and bring laurel to the university.
